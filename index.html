About RZ Commerce

From a Mathematics classroom in Multan to building Pakistan's own skincare brand online.

👑

Ramzan Baloch

Founder, RZ Commerce

📘 Facebook 📸 Instagram

My name is Ramzan Baloch — a BS Mathematics student at BZU Multan, and the founder of RZ Commerce.

This journey didn't start with money or resources. It started with a laptop, late nights, and a burning desire to learn. While studying complex equations during the day, I was teaching myself web development at night — HTML, CSS, JavaScript — one tutorial at a time, one error at a time.

I chose Glutawol because I believe every Pakistani deserves access to quality, authentic skincare — without overpaying or getting fooled by fake products. My mission is simple: deliver trust, deliver glow.

Today, when I see customers ordering, leaving feedback, and coming back — that moment makes every sleepless night worth it. We're still growing. Still learning. But we're here — and we're not stopping.
